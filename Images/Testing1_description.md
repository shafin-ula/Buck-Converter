## Testing 1

### Test Setup and Constraints

#### DC Power Supply
- **Vin:** 5 V  
  - Current limit: 5 mA
- **Vout:** 6 V  
  - Current limit: 5 mA

#### Waveform Generator
- **Signal Type:** PWM (Square Wave)
- **Frequency:** 50 kHz
- **Duty Cycle:** 50%
- **Amplitude:** 5 V peak-to-peak
- **DC Offset:** 2.5 V
- **Voltage Range:** 0 V to 5 V

> **Note:** The PWM signal must strictly swing between 0 V and 5 V.
